About

Phimmy Physics is a pixel-based particle sandbox simulation. Place materials, build machines, and watch physics unfold in real time.

Features include powder physics, fluid dynamics, fire propagation, tree growth, conveyor belts, grinders, lightning strikes, and more.

Version 1.0

Manual

Left Click - Place selected material

Right Click - Erase

Middle Click / Shift+Drag / CTRL+Click - Pan the view

Scroll Wheel - Adjust brush size

Shift + Scroll / CTRL + Scroll - Zoom in/out

Shift + Click (with material) - Draw straight line from start to release point

Conveyor - Moves particles above it in the selected direction. Places as a 5-wide belt asset.

Grinder - Grinds wood and leaves into sawdust, stone into gravel. Places as a 3-wide asset.

Lightning - Strikes from the sky down to cursor, destroying and igniting materials in its path.

Seed - Plant on dirt or stone to grow a tree over time.

Fire - Spreads to wood and leaves. Burns sawdust much faster than wood. Produces smoke.

Lava - Turns water into steam, ignites nearby flammable materials.
